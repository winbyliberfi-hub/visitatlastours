Meet Your Guide

Abdulaziz — Your Native Imazighen Guide

Born and raised in the foothills of the Atlas Mountains, Abdulaziz Aznag grew up speaking Tamazight, Arabic, and French before learning English to share his homeland with the world. His family has lived in these mountains for generations.

For Abdulaziz, every tour is personal. He doesn't just show you Morocco — he invites you into his culture, his villages, and the homes of his community. This is the authentic local experience that no big tour bus can replicate.
