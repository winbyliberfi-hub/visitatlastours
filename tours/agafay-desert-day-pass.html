🏜️ About This Experience

Trade the heat and chaos of the Marrakech medina for the serene beauty of the Agafay Desert. Just 40 minutes from the city, this unique stone desert is a world apart — a magical landscape of rolling rocky hills, olive groves, and uninterrupted silence.

Your day begins with a comfortable transfer from your hotel. Upon arrival at the luxury camp, you'll be welcomed with sweet mint tea and shown to your sun lounger beside a stunning pool that seems to float in the middle of the desert. Take a dip, sunbathe, read a book, or simply stare at the extraordinary landscape stretching to the horizon — this is the ultimate Moroccan day of relaxation.

At midday, sit down to a sumptuous 3-course traditional Moroccan lunch — freshly prepared with local ingredients, featuring harira soup, aromatic tagine with seasonal vegetables, and sweet Moroccan pastries to finish. Vegetarian options always available. This is the best of Morocco: stunning nature, exquisite food, and total peace of mind.

📍 Day Itinerary

10:00 AM
Hotel Pickup in Marrakech
Comfortable air-conditioned transfer from your hotel or riad. Scenic 40-minute drive through the Marrakech outskirts and across the beautiful Jbilet hills.
11:00 AM
Arrive at the Luxury Desert Camp
Welcome ceremony with fresh mint tea. Check in to your sun lounger, get your towels, and take in the spectacular panoramic views of the stone desert for the first time. Explore the camp, take photos, settle in.
11:00 AM – 1:00 PM
Pool Time & Desert Relaxation
Swim in the refreshing pool surrounded by desert hills and olive trees. Relax on your VIP sun lounger. The pool area offers stunning panoramic views of the Agafay — one of the most photogenic settings in all Morocco.
1:00 PM
Traditional 3-Course Moroccan Lunch
Sit down to a beautiful lunch feast: Harira soup or fresh salads, a choice of aromatic tagine (chicken, lamb, or vegetarian), followed by sweet Moroccan pastries and fresh fruit. Mint tea throughout.
2:00 – 4:00 PM
Free Time — Desert Exploration
More pool time, a short walk through the desert landscape, or simply relax in the shade and read. The Agafay is perfect for photography enthusiasts — the afternoon light on the rocky hills is absolutely magical.
4:00 PM — 5:00 PM
Return Transfer to Marrakech
Relaxed drive back to Marrakech. Drop-off at your hotel or riad, refreshed, sun-kissed, and satisfied.

❓ FAQ

Is the pool heated?

The pool is unheated, but it is refreshingly cool and perfect for hot Moroccan summer days. In cooler months (November–March), the pool can be a bit cool. Please check with us if swimming is important to you.

What should I bring?

Swimwear, sunscreen, sunglasses, a hat, comfortable sandals, and a change of clothes. Towels are provided at the camp. Bring a good book or camera for the desert views!

Are vegetarian options available for lunch?

Yes! A full vegetarian 3-course lunch is available. Please inform us when booking. Vegan options can also be arranged with advance notice.

Can I combine this with the Agafay Dinner experience?

Great idea! We can arrange a combined day/evening experience where you start with the day pass and pool, then stay on for the magical dinner, music, and fire show under the stars. Contact us for details!
